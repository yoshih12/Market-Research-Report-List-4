<p><p>Electro-Static Discharge (ESD) tape is essential for controlling static electricity in sensitive environments. The market is divided into single-sided and double-sided tape. Single-sided ESD tape features an adhesive on one side, ideal for securing items or shielding surfaces. In contrast, double-sided ESD tape offers adhesive on both sides, enabling bonding between surfaces without visible tape, which is useful for mounting or splicing applications. Both types are crucial for protecting electronic components from damage caused by static electricity.</p></p>
