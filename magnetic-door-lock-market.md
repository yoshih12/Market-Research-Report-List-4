<p><p>A magnetic door lock is a locking mechanism that operates using electromagnetism to secure doors. These locks are commonly used in commercial and residential buildings for their high level of security and ease of operation. The magnetic door lock consists of an electromagnet installed on the door frame and a metal armature plate attached to the door. When power is applied, the electromagnet attracts the armature plate, keeping the door securely locked.</p><p>The Magnetic Door Lock Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.6% during the forecast period.
