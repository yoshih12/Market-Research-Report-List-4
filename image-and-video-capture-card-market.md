<p><p>The image and video capture card market primarily features two types: PCI/PCIe capture cards and USB capture cards. PCI/PCIe capture cards are installed directly onto a computer's motherboard, offering high-performance video capture with low latency, making them ideal for professional streaming and gaming. Conversely, USB capture cards provide a portable solution that connects via USB ports, suitable for casual users and those seeking easy setup. Both types cater to diverse needs, from broadcasting to content creation and gaming applications.</p></p>
