<p><ul><li>CBTC</li><li>PTC</li><li>ATC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway signaling markets comprise three primary types: CBTC (Communications-Based Train Control), PTC (Positive Train Control), and ATC (Automatic Train Control). </p><p>CBTC utilizes real-time data communication to optimize train movements and minimize headways, enhancing efficiency and safety. PTC focuses on accident prevention by controlling train speeds and movements based on predetermined safety parameters. ATC automates train operations to ensure adherence to speed and signaling requirements, thereby improving safety and operational efficiency. Each system aims to enhance railway safety and performance in unique ways.</p></p>
