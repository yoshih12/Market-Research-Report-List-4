<p><p>The Electro Optical and Infrared Sensor market comprises two main types: hyperspectral and multispectral sensors. Hyperspectral sensors capture a wide range of wavelengths beyond visible light, enabling detailed analysis of materials and environmental conditions by detecting subtle spectral signatures. In contrast, multispectral sensors focus on a limited number of specific wavelength bands, allowing for effective monitoring of vegetation, land use, and atmospheric conditions. Both types are essential for applications in defense, agriculture, environmental monitoring, and remote sensing.</p></p>
