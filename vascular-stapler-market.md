<p><p>The vascular stapler market is categorized into electric and manual types. Electric vascular staplers are powered by a battery or electric source, offering consistent and precise stapling with minimal effort, suitable for complex surgeries. In contrast, manual vascular staplers require the surgeon to apply physical force to operate, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. Both types are essential in vascular surgeries for secure vessel closure, but their selection depends on the procedure's complexity and surgeon preference.</p></p>
