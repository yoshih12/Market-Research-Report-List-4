<p><strong>The Single Coated Medical Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Woven Single Coated Medical Tape</li><li>Non-woven Single Coated Medical Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single coated medical tape market comprises two main types: woven and non-woven single coated medical tapes. Woven single coated medical tapes are made from interlaced fibers, offering durability and breathability, making them suitable for long-term applications. Non-woven single coated medical tapes, on the other hand, are crafted from bonded fibers, providing a softer, more conformable option that is ideal for sensitive skin and post-operative use. Both types serve critical functions in wound care and medical applications.</p></p>
