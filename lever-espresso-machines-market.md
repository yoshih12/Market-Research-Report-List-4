<p><ul><li>Spring Type</li><li>Direct Lever Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lever espresso machine market is primarily divided into two types: spring type and direct lever type. Spring type lever machines utilize a built-in spring mechanism to create pressure, allowing for more consistent extraction with less physical effort from the user. In contrast, direct lever type machines rely on manual force applied by the user to generate pressure, offering greater control over the brewing process. Both types appeal to coffee enthusiasts seeking authentic espresso experiences, each with unique operational characteristics.</p></p>
