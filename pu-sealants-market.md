<p><p>PU sealants, or polyurethane sealants, are categorized into two main types based on their components: single-component and multi-component. Single-component sealants are ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for various applications. In contrast, multi-component sealants require the mixing of two or more components before application, offering enhanced customization and performance characteristics. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, and industrial settings for their durability and flexibility in sealing joints and gaps.</p></p>
