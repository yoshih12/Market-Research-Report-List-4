<p><p>The industrial masks market includes different types designed for specific protective needs. Valveless masks, also known as filter masks, provide a basic level of filtration without a breathing valve, making them suitable for environments with non-toxic particulate matter. In contrast, breather valve masks are equipped with one-way valves that allow exhaled air to escape, enhancing comfort during prolonged use and reducing heat buildup. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring worker safety in diverse environments.</p></p>
