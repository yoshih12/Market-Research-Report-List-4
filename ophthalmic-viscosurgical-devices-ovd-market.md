<p><p>Ophthalmic Viscosurgical Devices (OVDs) are specialized substances used in eye surgery to maintain space and protect tissues. The market is categorized into cohesive OVDs, which are thick and retain space effectively while providing stability during surgery; dispersive OVDs, which flow easily and provide a protective layer around sensitive tissues; and combined OVDs, which integrate the properties of both cohesive and dispersive types for enhanced performance. Each type addresses specific surgical needs to improve outcomes in ophthalmic procedures.</p></p>
