<p><p>Thermal Analysis Equipment market is segmented into various types such as Differential Scanning Calorimetry Analyzers, Thermogravimetric Analyzers, Simultaneous Thermal Analyzers, Differential Thermal Analyzers, Thermomechanical Analyzers, Dynamic Mechanical Analyzers, and Other. Differential Scanning Calorimetry Analyzers measure heat flow in materials, Thermogravimetric Analyzers measure changes in a material's weight as a function of temperature, and Simultaneous Thermal Analyzers combine multiple techniques in a single instrument. Differential Thermal Analyzers measure temperature difference between a sample and reference, while Thermomechanical Analyzers study mechanical properties. Dynamic Mechanical Analyzers measure changes in a material's mechanical properties under various conditions. The "Other" category includes miscellaneous thermal analysis equipment not covered by the other types.</p></p>
