<p><p>The super absorbent wound dressings market is categorized into two primary types: non-adhering dressings and self-adhering dressings. Non-adhering dressings are designed to prevent sticking to the wound, minimizing pain during removal and promoting healing. They often require a secondary dressing for support. In contrast, self-adhering dressings feature an adhesive layer that secures the dressing directly to the skin, providing convenience and stability. Both types effectively manage exudate, enhancing patient comfort and promoting optimal wound healing.</p></p>
