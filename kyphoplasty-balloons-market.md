<p><p>Kyphoplasty balloons are medical devices used in a minimally invasive procedure designed to treat vertebral compression fractures, often caused by osteoporosis. These balloons are inserted into the fractured vertebra and inflated to restore the vertebra's height and create space for bone cement, which stabilizes the fracture and alleviates pain. The increasing prevalence of osteoporosis among the aging population and a growing awareness of minimally invasive surgical options are driving the demand for kyphoplasty procedures and, consequently, kyphoplasty balloons.</p><p>The Kyphoplasty Balloons Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 6.70% during the forecast period.
