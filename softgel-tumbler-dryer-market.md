<p><ul><li>Standard Tumble Drying Systems</li><li>Continuous Tumble Drying Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Softgel Tumbler Dryer market includes two main types: Standard Tumble Drying Systems and Continuous Tumble Drying Systems. Standard Tumble Drying Systems operate in batch mode, where softgel capsules are loaded, dried, and unloaded periodically, allowing for flexibility in processing different batch sizes. In contrast, Continuous Tumble Drying Systems facilitate uninterrupted operation, providing a steady flow of softgels through the drying process, enhancing efficiency and reducing overall drying time. Both systems are critical for ensuring the quality and stability of softgel products.</p></p>
