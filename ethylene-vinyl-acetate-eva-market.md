<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) is classified based on its vinyl acetate (VA) content, affecting its properties and applications. </p><p>- **VA (up to 4%)**: This low VA content offers rigidity and is commonly used in films and coatings. </p><p>- **VA (4% to 30%)**: This mid-range VA provides a balance of flexibility and strength, making it suitable for footwear, adhesives, and packaging. </p><p>- **VA (greater than 40%)**: High VA content yields very flexible and soft materials, ideal for applications like medical supplies and high-performance seals.</p></p>
