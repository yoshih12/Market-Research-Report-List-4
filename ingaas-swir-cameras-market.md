SWIR area cameras capture images in a two-dimensional format, providing detailed information across a wide field of view. On the other hand, SWIR linear cameras produce images in a single line format, suitable for applications requiring high-speed scanning or inspection. Both types of cameras are widely used in various industries such as manufacturing, agriculture, and defense for their ability to see beyond what the human eye can perceive in the short-wave infrared spectrum.</p></p>
