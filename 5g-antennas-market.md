<p><ul><li>Switched Multi-beam Antennas</li><li>Adaptive Array Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G antennas market includes various types, notably switched multi-beam antennas and adaptive array antennas. Switched multi-beam antennas allow multiple coverage beams to be activated simultaneously, improving signal strength and capacity for several users. In contrast, adaptive array antennas dynamically adjust their beam patterns based on real-time environmental conditions, optimizing signal quality and reducing interference. Both types enhance wireless communication in 5G networks, supporting higher data rates and greater connectivity across diverse environments.</p></p>
