<p><p>The film packaging material market is primarily categorized into two types: rigid and flexible film packaging materials. Rigid film packaging consists of hard materials that maintain their shape, providing structural support and protection, often used for products like containers and trays. Conversely, flexible film packaging materials are pliable, allowing for customizable shapes and sizes, typically utilized for items such as pouches and bags. Both types serve crucial roles in various industries, enhancing product preservation and consumer convenience.</p></p>
