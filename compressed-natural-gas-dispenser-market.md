<p><strong>The Compressed Natural Gas Dispenser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Fast Fill"</li><li>"Time Fill"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) dispenser market includes two primary types: "Fast Fill" and "Time Fill." Fast Fill dispensers rapidly refuel vehicles, making them suitable for public stations and fleets requiring quick turnaround. They operate at high pressure and are ideal for busy environments. In contrast, Time Fill dispensers provide a slower refueling process, typically used overnight or during off-peak hours. This method is cost-effective for large fleets, allowing for optimized fuel use and reduced infrastructure costs.</p></p>
