<p><p>The Medical Temperature Management System market comprises two primary types: Patient Warming Systems and Patient Cooling Systems. Patient Warming Systems are designed to elevate a patient's body temperature, which is crucial in preventing hypothermia during surgeries or in critical care settings. Conversely, Patient Cooling Systems aim to lower body temperature, often employed in emergencies like heatstroke or to manage conditions such as traumatic brain injuries. Both systems are integral to maintaining optimal patient outcomes in various medical environments.</p></p>
