<p><ul><li>Lay-up</li><li>Compression Molding</li><li>Resin Injection</li><li>Resin Transfer Molding</li><li>Filament Winding</li><li>Pultrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy glass market encompasses various techniques for creating composite materials. Lay-up involves manually layering fiberglass sheets and epoxy resin. Compression molding uses heat and pressure to form parts in a mold. Resin injection injects liquid epoxy into molds for precise shapes. Resin transfer molding combines fiberglass and resin in a closed mold for efficiency. Filament winding applies continuous fibers saturated with resin around a mandrel. Pultrusion involves drawing fibers through resin and a heated die to create uniform profiles.</p></p>
