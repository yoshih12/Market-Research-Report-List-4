<p><strong>The Aluminium Die Casting Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Machine</li><li>Cold Chamber Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminium die casting machinery market consists of two main types of machines: hot chamber machines and cold chamber machines. Hot chamber machines are used for high volume production of small and medium-sized parts, as the molten metal is injected into the mold directly from a furnace. Cold chamber machines are suitable for larger parts and alloys that require higher melting temperatures, as the molten metal is poured into a separate chamber and then injected into the mold. Both types of machines have their own distinct advantages and applications in the die casting industry.</p></p>
