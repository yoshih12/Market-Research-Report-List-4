<p><p>The silicone oil market is divided into two main types: straight silicone oil and modified silicone oil. Straight silicone oil is a pure form, characterized by its inertness and thermal stability, making it ideal for applications in lubrication, coatings, and cosmetics. Modified silicone oil, on the other hand, has been chemically altered to enhance specific properties, such as viscosity or surface tension, catering to specialized applications in industries like automotive, electronics, and personal care. Both types are crucial for various industrial uses.</p></p>
