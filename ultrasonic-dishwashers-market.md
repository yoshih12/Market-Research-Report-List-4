<p><p>The ultrasonic dishwasher market can be categorized into two main types: embedded and water tank models. Embedded type ultrasonic dishwashers are integrated into kitchen cabinetry, utilizing ultrasonic waves for efficient cleaning while saving space. In contrast, water tank type ultrasonic dishwashers operate by immersing dishes in a water tank where ultrasonic waves dislodge dirt and grime. Both types leverage advanced ultrasonic technology to enhance cleaning performance and reduce water usage, catering to diverse consumer preferences and kitchen designs.</p></p>
