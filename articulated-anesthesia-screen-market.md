<p><p>Articulated Anesthesia Screens are medical devices used during surgeries to provide a barrier between the patient's head and the surgical field. There are two main types of Articulated Anesthesia Screens on the market: Mobile and Fixed. Mobile screens are portable and can be easily moved around the operating room, providing flexibility and convenience during procedures. Fixed screens are stationary and are typically mounted to the operating table or wall, offering stability and consistent positioning throughout surgeries. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining a sterile surgical environment.</p></p>
