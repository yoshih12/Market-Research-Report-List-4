<p><strong>The Electric Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Ball Valve</li><li>Electric Butterfly Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric valve market comprises various types, primarily focusing on electric ball valves and electric butterfly valves. Electric ball valves feature a spherical disc that provides quick shut-off and precise flow control, making them suitable for on/off applications. Electric butterfly valves, on the other hand, utilize a rotating disc to control fluid flow, ideal for throttling and regulating applications. Both valve types are increasingly adopted in industries like water treatment, oil and gas, and HVAC systems due to their efficiency and automation compatibility.</p></p>
