<p><ul><li>Single Turn</li><li>Multi-turn</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive absolute encoders market can be divided into two main types: single-turn and multi-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders measure the position within a single rotation, typically providing high accuracy and resolution within 360 degrees. In contrast, multi-turn encoders track multiple revolutions, using additional mechanisms to count turns beyond one rotation, making them suitable for applications where full rotation tracking is essential. Both types offer precise position measurement and are widely used in various industries for automation and control systems.</p></p>
