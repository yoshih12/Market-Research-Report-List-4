<p><p>Vehicle Event Data Recorders (EDRs) are devices that capture specific data during and prior to a vehicle crash, aiding in accident analysis and safety research. The market is divided into two types: independent and integrated. Independent EDRs are standalone systems installed separately from vehicle systems and offer versatility and compatibility across various vehicle models. Integrated EDRs, on the other hand, are embedded within the vehicle's existing systems, providing seamless data collection and real-time monitoring, enhancing vehicle safety features.</p></p>
