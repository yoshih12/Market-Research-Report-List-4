<p><p>Horizontal plate filters are commonly used in industrial processes to separate solids from liquids. The two main types are Reverse Flow Horizontal Plate Filters and Standard Horizontal Plate Filters. </p><p>Reverse Flow filters have the liquid flow in the opposite direction to gravity, while Standard filters have the liquid flow in the same direction as gravity. Reverse Flow filters are typically used for applications where a higher level of filtration efficiency is required, while Standard filters are more commonly used in general filtering applications.</p></p>
