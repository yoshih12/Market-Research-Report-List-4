<p><p>The plant nutrient soil market can be categorized into three types based on pH levels: acidic, alkaline, and neutral. Acidic soils, typically below pH 6, are often rich in minerals but may lack essential nutrients like calcium and magnesium. Alkaline soils, with a pH above 7, can hinder the availability of vital nutrients such as iron. Neutral soils, ranging from pH 6 to 7, provide an optimal environment for nutrient absorption, supporting balanced plant growth and productivity.</p></p>
