<p><ul><li>Three Phase</li><li>Two Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filter reactor markets are categorized into two primary types based on the phases involved: three-phase and two-phase. Three-phase filter reactors operate in a three-phase power system, effectively managing harmonic distortion and improving power quality across all three phases simultaneously. In contrast, two-phase filter reactors focus on two-phase systems, optimizing harmonic suppression and enhancing efficiency. Both types are essential for improving power factor and ensuring stable electrical performance in industrial applications, tailored to specific power system configurations and requirements.</p></p>
