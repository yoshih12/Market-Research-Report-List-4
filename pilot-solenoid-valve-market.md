<p><strong>The Pilot Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Way Solenoid Valve</li><li>Three-Way Solenoid Valve</li><li>Four-Way Solenoid Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pilot solenoid valve market is segmented into three main types: two-way, three-way, and four-way solenoid valves. Two-way solenoid valves control the flow of fluid in a single pathway, commonly used for on/off applications. Three-way solenoid valves manage the flow between two paths, allowing for mixing or diverting fluid. Four-way solenoid valves are designed for controlling actuators by directing flow to two different outlets, essential in systems requiring precise control of fluid and pressure.</p></p>
