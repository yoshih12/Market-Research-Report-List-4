<p><strong>The Flame Retardant Polyester Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inherent FRP Staple Fiber</li><li>Treated FRP Staple Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flame retardant polyester fiber market is primarily divided into two types: inherent FRP staple fiber and treated FRP staple fiber. Inherent FRP staple fiber is manufactured with built-in flame-retardant properties, making it resistant to ignition without any additional treatments. In contrast, treated FRP staple fiber undergoes a chemical process to enhance its flame-retardant characteristics, typically after production. Both types are widely utilized in applications such as textiles, upholstery, and industrial fabrics to enhance safety and compliance with fire regulations.</p></p>
