<p><strong>The LED Lamp Beads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD LED Lamp Bead</li><li>Directly-inserted LED Lamp Beads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED lamp beads market primarily consists of two types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) LED lamp beads and directly-inserted LED lamp beads. SMD LED lamp beads are compact components soldered directly onto circuit boards, offering high efficiency and flexibility in designs. They are widely used in modern lighting applications for their space-saving features. Conversely, directly-inserted LED lamp beads have leads that are inserted into a circuit board, providing easier installation and robust connections, making them suitable for traditional lighting fixtures.</p></p>
