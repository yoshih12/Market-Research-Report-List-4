<p><p>The polypropylene (PP) resin market includes various types catering to different applications. Homopolymer PP offers high rigidity and strength, making it suitable for packaging and automotive parts. High crystalline PP features enhanced thermal resistance, ideal for products requiring durability. Block copolymer PP combines flexibility and strength, making it suitable for impact-resistant applications. Random copolymer PP has improved clarity and chemical resistance, often used in consumer goods and medical devices. Each type serves distinct market needs, driving growth in diverse industries.</p></p>
