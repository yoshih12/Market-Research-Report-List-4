Second-trimester testing includes more comprehensive screenings and diagnostic tests conducted between 14 to 26 weeks. Third-trimester testing focuses on monitoring fetal growth and well-being in the final trimester. Post-term testing is done when a pregnancy extends beyond 42 weeks to assess the health of the baby and mother.</p></p>
