<p><p>Sulfur Coated Urea (SCU) is a slow-release nitrogen fertilizer that combines urea with a sulfur coating to enhance nutrient availability and reduce nitrogen leaching. Farmers are increasingly adopting SCU due to its benefits in improving soil fertility and crop yield while reducing the frequency of application. The market is further supported by a rising trend toward sustainable agriculture and environmental awareness, prompting the use of products that minimize chemical runoff.</p><p>In addition, innovations in coating technology and product formulations are enhancing the effectiveness of SCU, attracting more attention from growers.
