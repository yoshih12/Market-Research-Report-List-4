<p><p>The commercial vehicle battery market primarily features Flooded Lead-acid Batteries (FLAB) and Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) Batteries. FLABs are cost-effective, durable, and widely used due to their high power output and ability to handle deep discharges. AGM batteries, on the other hand, are maintenance-free, spill-proof, and offer better vibration resistance, making them ideal for demanding commercial applications. While FLABs are more affordable, AGM batteries provide superior performance, longer lifespan, and faster charging, catering to modern, high-efficiency commercial vehicles. Both types serve distinct needs in the market.</p></p>
