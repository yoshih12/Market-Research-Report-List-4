<p><p>Carton overwrapping machines are classified into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual machines require operators to wrap cartons by hand, suitable for low-volume production. Semi-automatic machines streamline the wrapping process but still necessitate some operator intervention, making them ideal for medium production needs. Automatic machines are fully automated, allowing for high-speed wrapping with minimal human involvement, catering to large-scale manufacturing operations. Each type serves different operational needs, balancing cost, efficiency, and production volume.</p></p>
