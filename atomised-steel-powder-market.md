<p><p>The atomised steel powder market is primarily categorized into two types based on the atomization technology used: water atomization and gas atomization. Water atomization involves using high-pressure water jets to create fine steel particles, resulting in powders with good flowability and uniformity, suitable for various applications. On the other hand, gas atomization utilizes high-velocity gas streams to produce finer and more spherical particles, offering superior mechanical properties, making it ideal for high-performance applications in aerospace and automotive industries.</p></p>
