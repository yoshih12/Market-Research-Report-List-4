<p><strong>The Hair Transplant Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT)</li><li>Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hair transplant services market is segmented into two types: Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). FUT involves the removal of a strip of scalp from the donor area, which is then divided into individual follicular units for transplantation. FUE, on the other hand, involves extracting individual hair follicles directly from the donor area without the need for a strip of scalp. Both methods are popular choices for those seeking to restore their hairline and achieve natural-looking results.</p></p>
