<p><p>The Milk SNF (Solid Not Fat) Analyzer market is primarily categorized into two types: Ultrasonic Milk Analyzers and Infrared Milk Analyzers. Ultrasonic analyzers utilize high-frequency sound waves to determine the density and composition of milk, providing accurate SNF measurements. In contrast, Infrared analyzers employ infrared light absorption principles to assess fat and SNF content quickly and non-destructively. Both technologies offer efficient solutions for dairy quality control, aiding producers in maintaining standards and enhancing product quality.</p></p>
