<p><strong>The X-ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode X-Ray Tube</li><li>Rotating Anode X-Ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray tube market is primarily divided into two types: stationary anode X-ray tubes and rotating anode X-ray tubes. Stationary anode tubes use a fixed target for X-ray production, making them suitable for lower-powered applications and cost-effective but less efficient for high-output needs. In contrast, rotating anode tubes feature a rotating target that allows for higher heat dissipation and greater X-ray output, making them ideal for advanced imaging techniques in hospitals and diagnostic centers.</p></p>
