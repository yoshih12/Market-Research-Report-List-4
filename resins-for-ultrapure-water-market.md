<p><strong>The Resins for Ultrapure Water Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cation Resin</li><li>Anion Resin</li><li>Mixed Bed Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrapure water market utilizes various types of resins, primarily cation, anion, and mixed bed resins. Cation resins exchange positive ions, effectively removing contaminants like calcium and magnesium, while anion resins target negative ions, eliminating impurities such as chlorides and sulfates. Mixed bed resins combine both cation and anion resins in a single system, providing comprehensive purification. These resins are essential for industries requiring high-purity water, such as pharmaceuticals, semiconductors, and power generation, ensuring minimal ionic contamination.</p></p>
