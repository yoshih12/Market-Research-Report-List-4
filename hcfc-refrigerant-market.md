<p><p>HCFC refrigerants, or Hydrochlorofluorocarbons, are a class of compounds used primarily in refrigeration and air conditioning systems. The market is experiencing a shift towards natural refrigerants and HFOs (Hydrofluoroolefins), which offer higher energy efficiency and lower environmental impacts. </p><p>Additionally, advancements in technology and increased awareness of environmental sustainability are influencing consumer choices and regulatory policies. As countries implement stricter legislation against HCFCs, the demand for substitute refrigerants is expected to rise.
