<p><strong>The Electric Propulsion System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gridded Ion Engine (GIE)</li><li>Hall Effect Thruster (HET)</li><li>High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thruster (HEMPT)</li><li>Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric propulsion system market comprises several types, each utilizing different mechanisms for thrust generation. Gridded Ion Engines (GIE) use electric fields to accelerate ions, offering high specific impulse. Hall Effect Thrusters (HET) utilize magnetic fields to ionize and accelerate propellant, providing efficiency and reliability. High Efficiency Multistage Plasma Thrusters (HEMPT) enhance thrust through multi-stage acceleration. Pulsed Plasma Thrusters (PPT) generate thrust via short plasma bursts. Other systems include innovations like laser propulsion and arcjet engines, expanding market diversity.</p></p>
