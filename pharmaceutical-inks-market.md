<p><ul><li>Water-based Pharmaceutical Inks</li><li>Solvent-based Pharmaceutical Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical inks market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based inks. Water-based pharmaceutical inks utilize water as the primary solvent, making them safer and more environmentally friendly, with lower toxicity and emissions. They are ideal for various applications, including packaging and labeling. In contrast, solvent-based pharmaceutical inks contain organic solvents, offering superior adhesion and durability on diverse surfaces, making them suitable for demanding environments. Each type caters to specific industry requirements, balancing performance and safety considerations.</p></p>
