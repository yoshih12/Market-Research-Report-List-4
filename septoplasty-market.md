<p><ul><li>Closed Septoplasty</li><li>Open Septoplasty</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The septoplasty market is divided into two main types: closed septoplasty and open septoplasty. Closed septoplasty involves internal incisions, allowing for minimal external scarring and faster recovery. It's typically preferred for simpler cases. In contrast, open septoplasty involves external incisions, providing better visualization and access to complex nasal structures, making it suitable for more severe deformities. The choice between the two methods depends on the specific anatomical challenges and the surgeon's expertise, impacting market demand and growth.</p></p>
