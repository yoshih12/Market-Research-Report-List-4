<p><p>The clip applicator market is divided into two main types: single-fire and multi-fire. Single-fire clip applicators are designed to discharge one clip at a time, offering precision for tasks requiring careful placement, ideal for smaller projects. In contrast, multi-fire clip applicators can discharge multiple clips in rapid succession, enhancing efficiency in larger-scale operations. The choice between these types depends on the specific application requirements, work volume, and the desired speed of operation in various construction and assembly environments.</p></p>
