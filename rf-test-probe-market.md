<p><p>The RF test probe market is categorized into two primary types: passive and active. Passive RF test probes do not amplify signals but instead rely on their design to transmit signals with minimal impact on the measured device, making them ideal for routine testing and measurement scenarios. In contrast, active RF test probes include built-in amplification, allowing them to measure weaker signals and enhancing performance in complex applications. Together, these probes enable effective RF signal analysis across various industries.</p></p>
