<p><p>The Active Toughening Agent for Epoxy Resin market includes two primary types: Rubber Toughening Agents and Thermoplastic Resin Toughening Agents. Rubber Toughening Agents enhance flexibility and impact resistance in epoxy formulations, effectively reducing brittleness. They are typically used to improve adhesion and durability. In contrast, Thermoplastic Resin Toughening Agents introduce ductility and toughness by incorporating thermoplastic materials, allowing the epoxy to absorb energy without fracturing. Together, these agents significantly improve the performance and longevity of epoxy resins in various applications.</p></p>
