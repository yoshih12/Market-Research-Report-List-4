<p><p>Security CMOS Image Sensors are categorized into two major types: Front-Side Illumination (FSI) and Back-Side Illumination (BSI) markets. FSI sensors have been predominant in the market for many years and are known for their cost-effective designs. On the other hand, BSI sensors have gained popularity due to their improved light sensitivity and better image quality. While FSI sensors are still widely used, the demand for BSI sensors is increasing in applications where high image quality is critical, such as security surveillance systems.</p></p>
