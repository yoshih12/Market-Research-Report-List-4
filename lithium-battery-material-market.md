<p><p>The lithium battery material market is primarily divided into two key types: cathode and anode materials. Cathode materials, such as lithium cobalt oxide and lithium iron phosphate, play a crucial role in determining battery energy density and stability. On the other hand, anode materials, typically graphite or lithium titanate, influence the charge/discharge rate and overall cycle life of the battery. Both segments are essential for enhancing the performance, efficiency, and longevity of lithium-ion batteries used in various applications.</p></p>
