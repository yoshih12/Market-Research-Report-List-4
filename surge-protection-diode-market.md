<p><strong>The Surge Protection Diode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ESD (Electro Static Discharge)</li><li>TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surge protection diode market encompasses two main types: ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) and TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression) devices. ESD diodes protect sensitive electronic components from voltage spikes caused by electrostatic discharge, safeguarding devices in consumer electronics and industrial applications. TVS diodes, on the other hand, provide rapid responses to transient voltage spikes, effectively clamping excess voltage during surges from sources like lightning or power fluctuations. Both types are crucial for enhancing the reliability and longevity of electronic circuits.</p></p>
