<p><ul><li>Active Chip</li><li>Passive Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protein biochip market comprises active and passive chips. Active chips are integrated with sensors or electronic components that can detect and analyze proteins in samples. They provide real-time data and are suitable for high-throughput screening. Passive chips, on the other hand, rely on external equipment for analysis and do not contain any electronic components. They are cost-effective and easier to manufacture, but require longer processing times. Both types of biochips play a crucial role in protein analysis for applications in research, diagnostics, and drug development.</p></p>
