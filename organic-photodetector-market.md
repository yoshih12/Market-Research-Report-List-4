<p><p>Organic Photodetectors can be classified into two main types: Photon Based and Thermal. Photon Based Organic Photodetectors rely on the interaction of photons with organic molecules to detect light. They are widely used in applications such as imaging, sensing, and communication systems. On the other hand, Thermal Organic Photodetectors utilize the heat generated by absorbed light to detect and convert it into an electrical signal. Both types have their own unique advantages and are used in various industries for light detection purposes.</p></p>
