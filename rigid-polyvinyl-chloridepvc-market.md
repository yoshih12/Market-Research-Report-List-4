<p><p>The rigid polyvinyl chloride (PVC) market is segmented into modified and unmodified uPVC. Modified uPVC incorporates additives to enhance properties like impact resistance, flexibility, and thermal stability, making it suitable for applications requiring durability, such as windows and piping. Unmodified uPVC, on the other hand, retains its original characteristics, offering excellent weather resistance and rigidity, primarily used in construction for doors, window frames, and plumbing systems. Both types cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs based on performance requirements.</p></p>
