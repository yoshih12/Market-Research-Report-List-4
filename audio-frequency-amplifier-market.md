Class B Amplifiers are more efficient but have higher distortion. Class AB Amplifiers combine the benefits of Class A and B. Class D Amplifiers are highly efficient but may have higher distortion levels at higher frequencies. Each type caters to different applications and user preferences in the audio frequency amplifier market.</p></p>
