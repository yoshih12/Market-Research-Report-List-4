<p><p>Photomasks are a critical component in the semiconductor manufacturing process. There are different types of photomasks used in the industry, such as EUV masks, UV masks, and DUV masks. EUV masks are used for extreme ultraviolet lithography, which enables higher resolution and accuracy in semiconductor fabrication. UV masks are utilized for ultraviolet lithography, while DUV masks are used for deep ultraviolet lithography, each offering different levels of precision and resolution in the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
