<p><p>The ship radar market is categorized into three main types: X-band, S-band, and others. X-band radars operate at higher frequencies, offering fine resolution and detail, making them ideal for navigation and tracking small vessels. S-band radars, with lower frequencies, provide longer range detection and are better suited for adverse weather conditions, often used for large vessel tracking. Other types may include specialized radars such as L-band or multifrequency systems, catering to specific maritime applications and requirements.</p></p>
