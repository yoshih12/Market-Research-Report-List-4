<p><strong>The Helicopter Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FFS and FTD</li><li>ATD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helicopter simulator market comprises three primary types: Full Flight Simulators (FFS), Fidelity Training Devices (FTD), and Aviation Training Devices (ATD). FFS represents the highest level of simulation, offering fully immersive experiences with realistic flight dynamics and cockpit environments. FTD provides moderate fidelity, focusing on specific training aspects without full motion capabilities. ATD, while offering basic simulation, often emphasizes procedural training and flight scenarios. Each type serves distinct training needs within the aviation sector, enhancing pilot proficiency and safety.</p></p>
