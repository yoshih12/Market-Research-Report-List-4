<p><p>The aircraft fuel tanks market is categorized into flexible and rigid tanks. Flexible tanks are lightweight, designed to conform to the aircraft's shape, and typically made from advanced composite materials for improved fuel efficiency and reduced weight. Rigid tanks, on the other hand, have a fixed structure, often integrated into the aircraft’s design, providing durability and reliability. Both types serve essential functions in fuel storage, with their selection depending on aircraft specifications, performance requirements, and operational needs.</p></p>
