<p><p>The medical imaging instrument market encompasses various technologies used for diagnostic purposes. X-ray devices utilize radiation to create images of bones and tissues. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) equipment employs strong magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images of organs and structures. Computed Tomography (CT) scanners combine multiple X-ray images for cross-sectional views. Ultrasound systems use sound waves to visualize soft tissues and fetal development. Nuclear imaging equipment involves radioactive substances to assess organ function and detect diseases.</p></p>
