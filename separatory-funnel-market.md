<p><p>The separatory funnel market can be categorized into three main types: spherical, pear, and tube. The spherical type features a round bottom, providing efficient separation and ease of cleaning. The pear type is characterized by a wider top and narrower neck, optimizing liquid separation and minimizing spillage. The tube type, with its elongated design, is suitable for small-scale separations in laboratory settings. Each type serves specific applications in chemistry, biology, and industrial processes, enhancing separation efficiency and user convenience.</p></p>
