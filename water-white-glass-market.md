<p><p>Water White Glass is a high-clarity, low-iron glass known for its exceptional transparency and minimal color distortion. In the market, it is available in two primary types: Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, creating a textured or patterned surface, often used in decorative applications. Float Glass, made by floating molten glass on molten tin, results in a smooth, flat surface, ideal for architectural and precision uses. Both types cater to industries requiring superior optical clarity and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
