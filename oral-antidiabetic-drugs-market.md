<p><p>Oral antidiabetic drugs market consists of various types of medications that help in managing blood sugar levels in patients with diabetes. Sulfonylureas are a type of drug that stimulates the release of insulin from the pancreas. Meglitinides work in a similar way by increasing insulin production. Alpha-glucosidase inhibitors slow down the absorption of carbohydrates in the intestine, thereby reducing post-meal blood sugar spikes. Each of these drug types plays a significant role in the management of diabetes.</p></p>
