<p><p>The Small Animal Imaging Equipment market comprises various imaging modalities used for research and diagnostic purposes. Optical imaging employs light to visualize biological processes, offering high-resolution images. Radionuclide imaging utilizes radioactive tracers for assessing metabolic activity. MRI provides detailed soft tissue images using magnetic fields. Computed Tomography (CT) combines X-ray images for cross-sectional views of internal structures. Ultrasound imaging uses sound waves to visualize organs and tissues, enabling real-time imaging and functional assessments in small animals.</p></p>
