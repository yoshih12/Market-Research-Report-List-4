<p><p>The VOC rotor market primarily features two types: zeolite and activated carbon. Zeolite rotors utilize natural minerals to adsorb volatile organic compounds (VOCs) efficiently, thanks to their high surface area and selective adsorption properties. In contrast, activated carbon rotors rely on carbon-based materials, also offering excellent VOC adsorption but with different regeneration characteristics. Both types are crucial in air pollution control, optimizing energy use and maintaining air quality across various industrial applications. Their effectiveness varies based on operational conditions and target pollutants.</p></p>
