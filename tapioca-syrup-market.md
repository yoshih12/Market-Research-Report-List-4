Tapioca maltodextrins, produced through enzymatic hydrolysis, serve as thickening agents and are often used in food processing for texture enhancement. Tapioca syrup solids are dehydrated versions, ideal for dry mixes and snacks, providing sweetness and improved shelf stability in a compact form.</p></p>
