<p><strong>The Belt Sampler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Sweep Sampler</li><li>Conventional Cross Stream Sampler</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Belt Sampler Market can be classified into three main types based on their sampling techniques. The Rotary Sweep Sampler involves a rotating cutter that sweeps across the conveyor belt to collect a sample. The Conventional Cross Stream Sampler collects a sample by diverting a portion of the material flow perpendicular to the main stream. Other types of samplers in the market may include stationary samplers or hybrid samplers that combine different sampling methods. Each type of sampler has its own advantages and limitations in terms of accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
