<p><p>Flax yarns market can be divided into two main types: wet spinning and dry spinning. Wet spinning involves passing the flax fibers through a solution before spinning them into yarn, resulting in a smoother and finer yarn. Dry spinning, on the other hand, involves spinning the flax fibers directly into yarn without the use of any solution, resulting in a slightly rougher texture. Both wet spinning and dry spinning methods cater to different market preferences and applications within the flax yarn industry.</p></p>
