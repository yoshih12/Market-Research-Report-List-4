<p><p>The acrylic-based pressure-sensitive adhesives market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based segments. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding properties and excellent performance in demanding environments, making them suitable for industrial applications. They typically have faster drying times but may contain volatile organic compounds. In contrast, water-based adhesives are more environmentally friendly, featuring lower emissions and safer handling. They are ideal for applications where ease of cleaning and safety are priorities, such as in packaging and labeling industries.</p></p>
