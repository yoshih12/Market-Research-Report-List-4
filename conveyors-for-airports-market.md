<p><p>In the airports market, conveyors play a crucial role in efficient baggage handling. Belt conveyors are widely used for transporting luggage on a continuous loop, accommodating various bag sizes while minimizing manual handling. Roller conveyors, on the other hand, utilize a series of rollers to facilitate smoother movement of heavy bags, allowing for quick transfers between different terminal areas. Both types enhance operational efficiency, reduce turnaround times, and improve overall passenger experience while ensuring safe and reliable baggage flow.</p></p>
