Ball type clutches have balls that disengage the drive when torque exceeds the limit, ensuring safety without wear. Other types may include electromagnetic or hydraulic clutches, which offer unique mechanisms for torque management. Each type serves specific applications, catering to varying operational requirements and safety standards.</p></p>
