<p><ul><li>Inherent FRP Staple Fiber</li><li>Treated FRP Staple Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flame Retardant Polyester Staple Fiber (FRP) market is primarily divided into two types: Inherent FRP Staple Fiber and Treated FRP Staple Fiber. Inherent FRP Staple Fiber contains flame-retardant properties built into the polymer structure, ensuring consistent performance under various conditions. In contrast, Treated FRP Staple Fiber undergoes a chemical treatment process to impart flame resistance. Both types are utilized in applications requiring fire safety, including textiles, insulation, and various industrial sectors, providing essential protection against fire hazards.</p></p>
