<p><p>Wastewater screening equipment is essential for removing debris and solids from wastewater. Auger screens use a rotating helical screw to lift and separate solids, ideal for fine screening. Bar screens consist of parallel bars or rods, effectively capturing larger debris, commonly used in preliminary treatment. Drum screens feature a rotating cylindrical mesh that filters out solids, suitable for medium to fine screening. Each type serves specific needs, balancing efficiency, maintenance, and application requirements in wastewater treatment processes.</p></p>
