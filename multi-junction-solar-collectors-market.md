<p><p>Multi-junction solar collectors can be classified into two main types: Concentrating Type Collectors and Non-Concentrating Type Collectors. Concentrating Type Collectors use lenses or mirrors to focus sunlight onto a smaller area of solar cells, increasing efficiency. Non-Concentrating Type Collectors, on the other hand, do not use any optical elements and instead absorb sunlight over a larger area. Both types have their own advantages and are utilized in various applications based on factors like space availability and desired energy output.</p></p>
