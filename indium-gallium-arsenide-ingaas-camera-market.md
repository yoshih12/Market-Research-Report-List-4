<p><p>The Indium Gallium Arsenide (InGaAs) camera market is divided into two main types: cooled and uncooled technology. Cooled InGaAs cameras utilize thermoelectric or cryogenic cooling systems to reduce sensor noise, enhancing sensitivity and allowing for high-performance imaging in challenging environments. These cameras are ideal for low-light applications. In contrast, uncooled InGaAs cameras operate at ambient temperatures, making them more compact and cost-effective, suitable for applications where extreme precision or low noise is less critical.</p></p>
