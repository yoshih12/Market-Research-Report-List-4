<p><strong>The Micro-Lens Arrays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aspherical Microlens Array</li><li>Spherical Microlens Array</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro-lens arrays market is segmented into aspherical and spherical microlens arrays. Aspherical microlens arrays feature non-spherical surfaces, allowing for improved optical performance by minimizing aberrations and enhancing light focusing capabilities. They are widely used in applications like advanced imaging systems and optical sensors. In contrast, spherical microlens arrays have uniformly curved surfaces, making them simpler to manufacture and suitable for general applications such as lighting and display technologies. Both types cater to diverse industries requiring precise optical management.</p></p>
