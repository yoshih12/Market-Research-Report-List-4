<p><p>The flow cytometry instrument market is categorized primarily into cell analyzers and cell sorters. Cell analyzers are designed for quantitative measurement of physical and chemical characteristics of cells, allowing for the analysis of numerous parameters simultaneously. In contrast, cell sorters not only analyze but also separate specific cell populations based on defined criteria, enabling further study or use in applications. Both types play crucial roles in research, clinical diagnostics, and therapeutic development, driving advancements in cellular analysis.</p></p>
