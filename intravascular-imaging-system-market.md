<p><p>The intravascular imaging system market consists of two primary types: Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) endoscopic imaging systems and hybrid intravascular imaging systems. OCT endoscopic imaging systems utilize light to create high-resolution images of blood vessels, aiding in the diagnosis and assessment of cardiovascular diseases. Hybrid intravascular imaging systems combine multiple imaging modalities, such as ultrasound and optical imaging, to provide comprehensive visualization and better diagnostic insights. Both types enhance procedural precision and patient outcomes in interventional cardiology.</p></p>
