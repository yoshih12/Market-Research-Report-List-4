<p><p>The veterinary blood pressure monitors market is divided into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic devices. Automatic monitors simplify the process by taking measurements with minimal user intervention, often using oscillometric technology for quick and accurate results. Semi-automatic monitors require some manual operation, such as inflating the cuff, but still provide electronic readings. Both types cater to the needs of veterinary professionals, offering varying levels of ease and precision, thereby enhancing animal care and diagnosis during veterinary visits.</p></p>
