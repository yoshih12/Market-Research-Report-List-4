<p><p>Roll-core transformers are essential components in electrical systems, categorized into two primary types: dry type and oil-immersed type. </p><p>Dry type transformers utilize air for cooling, making them suitable for indoor applications with limited space and lower environmental risks. Conversely, oil-immersed transformers use insulating oil for cooling and provide better thermal performance, making them ideal for outdoor installations and high-load applications. The choice between these types depends on factors such as installation location, environmental considerations, and required performance levels.</p></p>
