<p><p>The monoclonal antibodies sterile injectable drugs market is segmented into in vivo and in vitro production drugs. In vivo production drugs are generated within living organisms, often utilizing hybridoma technology to produce antibodies directly from immunized cells, resulting in highly specific and functional antibodies. In contrast, in vitro production drugs are created outside of living organisms, typically using recombinant DNA technology and cell cultures, allowing for precise control over the antibody characteristics and large-scale production. Both types are critical for therapeutic applications.</p></p>
