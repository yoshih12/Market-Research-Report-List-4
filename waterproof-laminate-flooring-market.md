<p><p>Waterproof laminate flooring comes in various types, with the most common being Three Layer and Multilayer. Three Layer waterproof laminate flooring consists of a core layer, a pattern layer, and a wear layer. This type is typically more affordable and easier to install. Multilayer waterproof laminate flooring, on the other hand, features multiple core layers for added durability and stability. This type is more resistant to moisture and wear and tear, making it ideal for high-traffic areas.</p></p>
