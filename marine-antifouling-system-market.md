<p><p>Marine antifouling systems are essential to prevent the accumulation of marine organisms on boat hulls. Ultrasound antifouling systems use sound waves to deter the attachment of organisms, while electrolytic antifouling systems release ions to create a hostile environment. Both types of systems are eco-friendly alternatives to traditional toxic antifouling methods. Ultrasound systems are known for their energy efficiency and minimal maintenance requirements, while electrolytic systems are effective in controlling biofouling in both freshwater and saltwater environments.</p></p>
