The demand for SWIR cameras is also being driven by their superior capabilities compared to visible light cameras, such as the ability to see through fog, smoke, and other visual obstructions.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the SWIR InGaAs area scan cameras market is the development of compact and cost-effective cameras that offer high-resolution imaging capabilities.
