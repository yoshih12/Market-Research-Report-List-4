<p><strong>The Hermetic Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passivation Glass</li><li>Ceramic-Metal</li><li>Reed-type Glass</li><li>Glass-Metal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hermetic containers can be classified into four main types based on the material used for sealing purposes: passivation glass, ceramic-metal, reed-type glass, and glass-metal containers. Passivation glass containers involve using a layer of glass to seal the container hermetically. Ceramic-metal containers utilize a combination of ceramic and metal for sealing purposes. Reed-type glass containers use a reed switch to create a hermetic seal. Glass-metal containers involve sealing the container using a combination of glass and metal materials. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications.</p></p>
