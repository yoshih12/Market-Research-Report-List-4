<p><p>Ambulatory EHR systems are categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise systems are installed locally on a healthcare provider's servers, offering greater control over data and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based EHR systems are hosted remotely, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, while ensuring easy access and automatic updates. Both options cater to the needs of outpatient services, influencing cost, security, and user experience differently.</p></p>
