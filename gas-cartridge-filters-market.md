<p><p>The gas cartridge filters market encompasses two main types: surface filters and depth filters. Surface filters capture particles on their outer layer, providing efficient filtration for larger contaminants and often featuring a longer lifespan. Depth filters, on the other hand, trap particles throughout the filter medium, allowing for effective filtration of smaller contaminants. These filters are crucial in various applications, including industrial processes and air purification, ensuring clean gas outputs and enhancing operational efficiency across multiple sectors.</p></p>
