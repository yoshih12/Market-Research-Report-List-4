<p><p>Acrylic-based waterborne coatings are available in two primary forms: liquid and powder. Liquid coatings are typically ready to use and can be easily applied through various methods such as spray, brush, or roll. They dry quickly, providing a smooth finish and high durability. Powder coatings, on the other hand, are applied in a dry form and cured through heat, resulting in a robust and even finish. Both types offer environmentally friendly options with low volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them popular in various applications.</p></p>
