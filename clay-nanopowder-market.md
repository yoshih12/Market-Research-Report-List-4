<p><p>The clay nanopowder market is divided into two main types: kaolinite and smectite. Kaolinite, known for its fine particle size and high purity, is widely used in ceramics, paper, and cosmetics. Smectite, characterized by its swelling properties and high adsorption capacity, finds applications in pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and environmental remediation. Both types offer unique properties, influencing their use in various industries, while the growing demand for eco-friendly materials and advanced applications drives market expansion.</p></p>
