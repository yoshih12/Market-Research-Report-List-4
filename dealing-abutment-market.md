<p><strong>The Dealing Abutment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Abutments</li><li>Angled Abutments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Straight abutments are used in situations where the angle of the implant matches the natural angle of the tooth. They provide a straight path for the crown to be placed on top of the implant. Angled abutments, on the other hand, are used when the angle of the implant does not align with the natural angle of the tooth. They allow for the crown to be placed at a more natural angle, improving aesthetics and functionality. Both types of abutments cater to different market preferences and needs.</p></p>
