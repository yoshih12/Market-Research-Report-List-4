<p><strong>The Residential Water Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point of Entry (POE)</li><li>Point of Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential water treatment market is categorized into two main types: Point of Entry (POE) and Point of Use (POU). POE systems treat water at the main supply line, ensuring that all water entering the home is filtered and purified, which benefits appliances and plumbing. Conversely, POU systems filter water at specific outlets, such as faucets or under-sink units, focusing on providing clean drinking water directly where it's consumed. Both types address different consumer needs for water quality and safety.</p></p>
