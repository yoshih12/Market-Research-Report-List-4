<p><strong>The Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter</li><li>Passive Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter market is divided into two main types: Active and Passive dosimeters. Active Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeters continuously monitor and display radiation levels in real-time, allowing users immediate feedback and prompting for appropriate safety measures. In contrast, Passive Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeters measure radiation exposure over a period but do not provide real-time data; they require analysis after exposure. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring safety in environments with potential radiation hazards.</p></p>
