<p><p>Metamaterial antennas can be categorized into two main types: Transmitting Antennas and Receiving Antennas. Transmitting antennas are designed to efficiently radiate electromagnetic waves into the surrounding medium, allowing for effective communication. On the other hand, Receiving antennas are built to capture incoming electromagnetic waves and convert them into electrical signals for processing. Both types of antennas utilize metamaterials to enhance their performance, such as increasing bandwidth, improving radiation patterns, and reducing size.</p></p>
