<p><ul><li>Macroscopic Urinalysis</li><li>Biochemical Urinalysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Urinalysis test market is comprised of two main types: Macroscopic Urinalysis and Biochemical Urinalysis. Macroscopic Urinalysis involves the visual examination of urine for color, clarity, odor, and any abnormal substances like blood or crystals. This type of test helps in diagnosing conditions like urinary tract infections or kidney stones. On the other hand, Biochemical Urinalysis involves testing urine for levels of specific substances like protein, glucose, or ketones, which can indicate conditions such as diabetes or kidney disease. Both types of urinalysis play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various health conditions.</p></p>
