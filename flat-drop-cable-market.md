<p><ul><li>Single Mode Cable</li><li>Multi Mode Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flat drop cables are designed for outdoor use, primarily in fiber optic telecommunications. The single-mode cable features a small core that allows light to travel directly down the fiber, making it ideal for long-distance communication with minimal signal loss. In contrast, multi-mode cables have a larger core supporting multiple light paths, suited for shorter distances within buildings or campuses. Both types cater to diverse connectivity needs, with single-mode cables emphasizing long-range efficiency and multi-mode cables focusing on high-capacity local networking.</p></p>
