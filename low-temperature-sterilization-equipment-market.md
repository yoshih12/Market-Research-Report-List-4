<p><p>Low temperature sterilization equipment encompasses various methods to effectively eliminate microorganisms from heat-sensitive medical devices. Ethylene Oxide Sterilization utilizes gas to penetrate materials, making it suitable for complex instruments. Vaporized Hydrogen Peroxide Sterilization employs vaporized hydrogen peroxide, offering a safer alternative with quicker cycle times. Nitrogen Dioxide Sterilization uses nitrogen dioxide gas, known for its efficacy in killing spores and bacteria. Other methods may include ozone sterilization and radiation techniques, catering to diverse healthcare needs and applications.</p></p>
