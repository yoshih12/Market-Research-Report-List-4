<p><p>The Sledge Microtomes market is categorized into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual microtomes require the operator to physically slice samples, offering precise control but demanding considerable skill and time. Semi-automatic microtomes combine manual operation with mechanized assistance, enhancing efficiency while still requiring some operator input. Automatic microtomes fully mechanize the slicing process, increasing throughput and consistency, making them ideal for high-volume laboratories. Each type serves different user needs based on precision, throughput, and operator experience.</p></p>
