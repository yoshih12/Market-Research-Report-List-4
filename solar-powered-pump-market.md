<p><strong>The Solar-powered Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Suction</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar-powered pump market comprises two main types: surface suction and submersible pumps. Surface suction pumps are installed above ground and draw water from a source through suction, suitable for shallow water bodies. In contrast, submersible pumps are designed to be submerged in the water source, pushing water to the surface, making them ideal for deeper wells and boreholes. Both types efficiently utilize solar energy, promoting sustainability in irrigation, livestock watering, and other applications.</p></p>
