<p><ul><li>DIN Rail Mount</li><li>Panel Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic timer switches are available in two main market types: DIN rail mount and panel mount. DIN rail mount timer switches are designed to be mounted on a standard DIN rail in an electrical panel, making them ideal for industrial applications where space is limited. Panel mount timer switches, on the other hand, are designed to be directly mounted on a control panel or enclosure, making them more suitable for commercial and residential applications where aesthetics and ease of access are important.</p></p>
