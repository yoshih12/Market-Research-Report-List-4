<p><p>Breast imaging technology market includes different types of technologies such as Molecular Breast Imaging (MBI), Positron Emission Tomography-Computed Tomography (PET-CT), and Positron Emission Mammography (PEM). MBI uses a radioactive tracer to identify potential tumors in the breast. PET-CT combines functional imaging with anatomical information to detect abnormalities. PEM provides high-resolution images of the breast for improved detection of cancer. Other markets in breast imaging technology include ultrasound, MRI, and thermography, offering various options for breast cancer detection and diagnosis.</p></p>
