<p><strong>The Blood Pressure Disorders Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diuretics</li><li>Angiotensin Converting Enzyme (ACE) Inhibitors</li><li>Angiotensin Receptor Blockers (ARBs)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood pressure disorders drug market comprises several key types that target hypertension management. Diuretics help eliminate excess fluid, reducing blood volume. Angiotensin Converting Enzyme (ACE) inhibitors lower blood pressure by inhibiting the conversion of angiotensin I to angiotensin II, a potent vasoconstrictor. Angiotensin Receptor Blockers (ARBs) directly block the effects of angiotensin II, promoting vasodilation. Additionally, "Others" encompasses various drug classes like beta-blockers and calcium channel blockers, each contributing uniquely to blood pressure control.</p></p>
