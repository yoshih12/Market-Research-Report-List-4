<p><p>A Fluorescent Fundus Camera is a specialized device used to capture high-resolution images of the back of the eye, specifically the retina and the optic nerve head. The increasing prevalence of eye diseases, growing geriatric population, and advancements in imaging technology are driving the demand for these cameras. Additionally, the rising awareness about the importance of early detection and treatment of eye diseases is also contributing to market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the Fluorescent Fundus Camera Market include the development of portable and handheld devices for point-of-care testing, integration of artificial intelligence for image analysis, and the introduction of advanced imaging techniques such as optical coherence tomography.
