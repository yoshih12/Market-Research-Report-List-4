<p><p>The baby carrier market encompasses three main types: buckled baby carriers, baby wrap carriers, and baby sling carriers. Buckled baby carriers feature adjustable straps and buckles for easy use and support, making them suitable for longer outings. Baby wrap carriers consist of long pieces of fabric that wrap around the caregiver and baby, providing a customizable fit and intimate closeness. Baby sling carriers are quick to wear, with a looped fabric that allows for easy nursing and carrying, offering a relaxed and comfortable experience.</p></p>
