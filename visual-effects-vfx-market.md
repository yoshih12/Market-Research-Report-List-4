<p><p>Visual Effects (VFX) market is divided into various types such as Simulation FX, Animation, Modelling, Matte Painting, and Compositing. Simulation FX involves creating realistic simulations of natural phenomena like fire, water, and smoke. Animation involves bringing characters and objects to life through motion. Modelling is the process of creating 3D models of objects and characters. Matte Painting is the art of creating digital backgrounds for films. Compositing involves combining various visual elements to create a final image or scene. Each of these types plays a crucial role in the overall VFX industry.</p></p>
