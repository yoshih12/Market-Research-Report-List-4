<p><strong>The Dissolved Oxygen Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical (Luminescent) Type</li><li>Clark Electrochemical (Amperometric) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dissolved Oxygen Sensors market encompasses two main types: Optical (Luminescent) and Clark Electrochemical (Amperometric) sensors. Optical sensors use luminescence technology to measure oxygen levels, providing accurate and stable readings with minimal maintenance. In contrast, Clark Electrochemical sensors operate on an electrochemical principle, measuring current changes to determine dissolved oxygen concentration. Both types are widely utilized in water quality monitoring, aquaculture, and various industrial applications, each offering distinct advantages depending on the specific requirements of the environment and application.</p></p>
