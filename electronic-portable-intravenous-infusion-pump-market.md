<p><p>The electronic portable intravenous infusion pump market includes two primary types: constant volume control type and syringe injection type. The constant volume control type delivers a precise and consistent flow rate of fluids, ensuring accurate medication administration over time. In contrast, the syringe injection type utilizes a syringe mechanism, allowing for variable flow rates and precise dosing, making it suitable for smaller liquid volumes. Both types facilitate efficient patient care through controlled and safe intravenous therapy in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
