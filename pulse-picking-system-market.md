<p><p>Pulse Picking System Market Types include Electro-optic Modulator and Acousto-optic Modulator. Electro-optic Modulators use the electro-optic effect to control the polarization of light, allowing for efficient pulse picking. Acousto-optic Modulators use acoustic waves to diffract light, enabling precise control over the timing and intensity of pulses. Both technologies are widely used in applications such as laser systems, telecommunications, and scientific research where precise pulse control is essential.</p></p>
