<p><ul><li>Autoclave Process</li><li>Tubular Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDPE granule market is primarily segmented into two production processes: the Autoclave Process and the Tubular Process. The Autoclave Process involves high-pressure reactors that facilitate polymerization, resulting in high-density polyethylene (HDPE) with robust mechanical properties. In contrast, the Tubular Process utilizes a continuous flow system for polymer production, enabling higher efficiency and better thermal management. Both methods cater to diverse applications, impacting product characteristics and market demand dynamics for HDPE granules in various industries.</p></p>
