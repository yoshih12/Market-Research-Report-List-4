<p><strong>The Marine Electronic Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vector Chart (ENC)</li><li>Raster Chart (RNC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Electronic Navigation System market encompasses various types of charting technologies, primarily Vector Charts (ENC) and Raster Charts (RNC). Vector Charts are digital maps that use mathematical expressions to represent navigational information, providing greater flexibility and detail, while also allowing for real-time updating. In contrast, Raster Charts are essentially scanned images of paper nautical charts, maintaining traditional formats but lacking dynamic features. Both chart types serve critical roles in enhancing maritime navigation safety and efficiency.</p></p>
