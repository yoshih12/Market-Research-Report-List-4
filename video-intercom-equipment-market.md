<p><p>The video intercom equipment market is primarily divided into two types: analog and IP-based systems. Analog video intercoms use traditional wiring and provide basic audio and video communication, making them more cost-effective but limited in features. Conversely, IP video intercoms utilize internet protocols, offering high-definition video, advanced functionalities, remote access, and integration with smart home systems. While analog systems cater to budget-conscious users, IP models appeal to those seeking enhanced connectivity and capabilities in modern security solutions.</p></p>
